* What is generative art?

To me, the art is the algorithm that creates some space of artistic output. The quality of a generative artwork is determined by the quality of the output space it is capable of producing. As the artist, my role is to write code that controls this space but still produces diverse and interesting works.

* Are these AI/ChatGPT/Midjourney/Sora/StableDiffusion generated?

No, it's all hand coded using p5.js, a JavaScript library for creative coding.
